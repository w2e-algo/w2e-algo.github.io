Project Basic Information

The project turns biogas hub design into an applied teaching and learning framework for sustainable systems modelling.

The framework supports decisions about regional biogas hub location, feedstock allocation, feedstock blending, transport logistics, resource use, energy pricing, and emissions reduction under uncertainty.

Framework Focus

  • Generate realistic data for robust waste-to-energy experimentation.
  • Predict biogas yield, resource demand, digestate output, and market conditions.
  • Optimise hub locations, feedstock allocation, blending, transport, and operating inputs.
  • Explore uncertainty from feedstock supply, energy prices, carbon value, logistics, and performance risk.
